📰 本文 + 和越対訳

中国国家統計局は15日、2026年4〜6月期の国内総生産（GDP）が前年同期比4.3%増だったと発表した。直前の1〜3月期は5.1%増で、成長率は明確に減速している。中国政府が年間目標として掲げる「5%前後」の成長を下回ったことで、景気刺激策のさらなる強化が求められる可能性が高まっている。背景には、米国との関税摩擦による輸出の落ち込みや、不動産市場の低迷が続いていることがある。内需喚起に向けた補助金政策は一定の効果を示しているものの、外部環境の悪化が全体の押し下げ要因となった。国際通貨基金（IMF）も中国の成長見通しを下方修正しており、世界経済への影響も注視されている。

Cục Thống kê Quốc gia Trung Quốc công bố ngày 15 rằng GDP quý 2 năm 2026 tăng 4,3% so với cùng kỳ năm trước — thấp hơn mức 5,1% của quý 1. Nguyên nhân chính là xuất khẩu giảm do căng thẳng thuế quan với Mỹ và thị trường bất động sản vẫn trì trệ. Các chính sách trợ cấp kích cầu nội địa có hiệu quả nhất định nhưng môi trường bên ngoài vẫn tạo áp lực lớn. IMF cũng đã hạ dự báo tăng trưởng của Trung Quốc, và thế giới đang theo dõi tác động tới kinh tế toàn cầu.

C-03The Tower Keeps Rising — Armin Ronacher on How AI Agents Accelerate Code While Dissolving Team Coordination

📰 Article (English)

Armin Ronacher, creator of Flask and Jinja2, published an essay drawing a parallel between Bruegel's Tower of Babel and modern AI-assisted software engineering. His central argument: the biblical catastrophe was not a loss of technical capability but a loss of shared language — "the people is one, and they have all one language" was their true source of power. Large software projects similarly depend not on individual coding speed but on collective understanding of architecture, ownership, and design intent. This shared knowledge historically emerged through friction: code reviews forced developers to learn adjacent systems; coordination requirements created incidental knowledge transfer. AI coding agents eliminate this friction. Developers can independently request features without reading surrounding code or consulting teammates. Individual changes remain syntactically sound and tests pass, but the synchronizing mechanism that maintained architectural coherence is gone. Unlike Babel — where communication collapse halted construction — AI-assisted systems continue generating code even after collective understanding dissolves. The tower does not fall, so the loss goes unnoticed. It just keeps rising, until the accumulated invisible debt makes meaningful change impossible. Ronacher is not anti-AI; he advocates for deliberate preservation of coordination rituals that AI otherwise quietly erodes.
