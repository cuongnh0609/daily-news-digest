📰 本文 + 和越対訳

Kết luận chính: kiểm soát môi trường (environment controls) đáng tin cậy hơn model-layer defense đối với các rủi ro nghiêm trọng nhất.

B2AWS、ブラウザから使えるコーディングAIエージェント「Kiro Web」をプレビュー公開――spec-firstのautoモードを搭載

📰 Article (English)

Let's Encrypt announced it will adopt Merkle Tree Certificates (MTCs) as its post-quantum authentication strategy, targeting a staging rollout in late 2026 and production in 2027. The announcement solves a fundamental problem that has blocked PQ adoption in TLS: post-quantum signatures are simply too large. ML-DSA-44, the leading PQ signature standard, produces signatures of ~2,420 bytes compared to RSA-2048's 256 bytes and ECDSA-P256's 64 bytes.
