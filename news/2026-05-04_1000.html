📰 本文 + 和越対訳

Một kỹ sư muốn tự làm MCP server đã đụng phải bức tường xác thực và quyết định học lại OAuth 2.1 từ đầu, sau đó công bố kết quả trên Zenn dưới dạng sách kỹ thuật miễn phí 10 chương. 📰 Article (English)

Software engineer Lars Faye published a provocative counterpoint to the prevailing "spec-driven development" narrative, arguing that fully agentic coding workflows create measurable long-term risks: cognitive debt, skill atrophy, and a self-defeating paradox. Unlike past transitions (assembly to higher-level languages), the skills degradation from AI coding agents is already documented and measurable within just a few years of widespread use. The core contradiction Faye identifies is what he calls the "paradox of supervision": agentic coding actively degrades the very skills needed to effectively oversee and review agentic coding. His proposed approach: use LLMs to generate specs and plans, code 20–100% manually depending on task complexity, and never ask an LLM to implement something you haven't done before or couldn't do yourself.
