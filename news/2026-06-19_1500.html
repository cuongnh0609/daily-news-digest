The Model Context Protocol (MCP) has shipped a major enterprise authentication upgrade called Enterprise-Managed Authorization, built on the Identity Assertion JWT Authorization Grant (ID-JAG) standard. Security teams gain centralized, auditable control over which employees can access which MCP tools, preventing personal and work account mixing. This effectively removes enterprise authentication as a barrier to MCP adoption at scale.
