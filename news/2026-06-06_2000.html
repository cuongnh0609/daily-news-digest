B1Claude で Gemini 議事録から決定事項・TODO を自動抽出——Slack・Jira 連携まで一気通貫のワークフロー

📰 本文 + 和越対訳

Google MeetのGemini自動議事録機能は、会議後にドキュメントとして保存される一方で、「何が決まったのか」「次に誰が何をするのか」を追跡するには手動での整理が必要だった。株式会社TOKIUMのエンジニアはこの課題を解決するため、Claude Skills（Claude Codeのスキル機能）を活用したワークフローを構築した。Google Docsの議事録をMarkdownに変換してGitHubリポジトリに保存し、スケジュールトリガーでClaudeが決定事項・TODO・担当者・期限を抽出、SlackとJiraに自動投稿する仕組みだ。重要なのはAIへの指示（プロンプト）をSkills化することで再利用可能にしている点で、同じ構造の議事録なら毎回同じ品質で情報抽出できる。実装にはClaude Code CLI、Google Apps Script、GitHub Actionsを組み合わせており、会議後の情報整理コストを大幅に削減できたと報告している。

Tính năng ghi biên bản tự động của Gemini trong Google Meet tạo ra tài liệu sau mỗi cuộc họp, nhưng để theo dõi "điều gì đã được quyết định" và "ai sẽ làm gì" vẫn cần chỉnh sửa thủ công. 📰 Article (English)

This 26-minute in-depth guide by 0xkato, which topped Hacker News with 385 points, demystifies how transformer-based large language models function by tracing nine core mechanisms in sequence. The article begins with tokenization — how raw text is split into sub-word units — then moves through embedding (mapping tokens to high-dimensional vectors), positional encoding (injecting sequence order), and the self-attention mechanism (enabling each token to "look at" all others to determine relevance). Multi-head attention allows the model to track multiple types of relationships simultaneously. Feed-forward networks handle per-token processing in parallel, while residual connections ensure gradients flow stably through many layers. Layer normalization stabilizes training. Finally, next-token prediction ties everything together: each forward pass produces a probability distribution over the vocabulary, from which the next token is sampled. The piece makes a sharp observation: most state-of-the-art LLMs — GPT-4, Gemini, Claude, Llama — share this fundamental architecture. Differences in capability stem from training data, scale, and post-training alignment, not divergent designs. "The architecture is largely settled," the author writes, shifting the frontier to training recipes and alignment techniques.
