The session log doubles as external memory, letting Claude interrogate past events without burning context window space — a critical enabler for long-horizon agentic tasks. The post describes how this design enables multiple "brains" operating independently and multiple "hands" (execution environments) per brain, with flexible deployment across VPCs and infrastructure boundaries.
