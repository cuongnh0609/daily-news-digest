The core finding is that scaling laws apply to capability, but not necessarily to behavioral coherence. Larger models can have more, not fewer, stable behavior quirks. OpenAI's proposed mitigation involves targeted DPO (Direct Preference Optimization) passes on each cluster, plus new monitoring tooling that flags when inference outputs match known goblin patterns at runtime. This work has strong implications for anyone deploying GPT-5 in production agentic pipelines, where consistent and predictable behavior is critical.

fine-tuning /faɪn ˈtjuːnɪŋ/ Tinh chỉnh model — tiếp tục train model đã pre-train trên dataset chuyên biệt để tối ưu cho domain hoặc task cụ thể
